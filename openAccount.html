Link Custody Account

  • Print this page
  • Help

A linked custody account replicates the trades of another user's custody account, i.e. any trades or changes to the portfolio that user performs, will also be executed on your portfolio.

You need to know the number of the custody account you wish to link to.
  • Automated tax income statement
  • Free of arbitrage
  • Annual fee instead of Capital Gains Tax
